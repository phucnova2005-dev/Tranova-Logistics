General Storage Solutions

Secure, Scalable, and Cost-Effective

Overview

Our General Storage solution is designed for standard cargo, dry goods, furniture, and consumer electronics. We utilize a Selective Pallet Racking System that allows 100% accessibility to every pallet.

Key Features

  • Flexible Rental: Pay only for the space you use (per m³ or per pallet/month).
  • Inventory Management: All goods are barcoded upon entry.
  • Safety Standards: Equipped with industrial-grade sprinklers and 24/7 CCTV.

The Process

  1. Inbound: Goods receipt, quality check, and barcoding.
  2. Put-away: System-directed placement into optimal locations.
  3. Outbound: Order picking and handover to transporters.
